Master Plan for Tiruppur Local Planning Area

Planning Proposal

PROPOSALS FOR NEW ROADS

  • Ring Roads – connecting the Radial pattern
  • Ring 1 – 60 ft road (Thirumuruganpoondi, Andipalayam, Chettipalayam, Neruperichal)
  • Ring 2 – 150 ft road (Sullikadu, Avanashi, Goundampalayam, Polikalipalayam)
  • Ring 3 – 100 ft road (Chengapalli, Thekkalur, Chitambalam, Avinashipalayam)
  • Sub Arterial Roads - distributes traffic to lower leve

PROPOSALS FOR TRANSPORTATION HUBS

  • 4 Truck Terminals are proposed along the ring roads reducing the goods traffic in the core area of the town.
  • 3 new bus stands and 1 Bus Stand with Bus Depot is proposed for decentralizing the Bus stands in the core LPA.
  • Existing Tiruppur Railway Station is proposed to be revamped for a cost of 22 crores
  • 2 New Railway Stations are proposed to be constructed increasing the possibility of Suburban Transit System along the Coimbatore – Salem Railway Corridor.

PROPOSALS FOR BRTS NETWORK

  • BRTS corridor along the 2nd Ring Road, utilizing its substantial width of 150 feet.

PROPOSALS FOR NON-MOTORIZED TRANSPORT

Non-Motorized Transport (NMT) corridor
  • In North, NMT proposed along 2nd Ring Road and the road adjacent to Noyyal River
  • In South, NMT proposed along Palladam road, 2nd ring road and Dharapuram road enabling the students and workers along the route for NMT.

PROPOSALS FOR RUB/ROB, RIVER BRIDGES

  • Road over Bridge, Road Under Bridge are Proposed along the railway line alignment
    • 13 Road Under Bridge are proposed to be widening
    • 5 Road Over Bridge are Proposed to be Widening
  • River Bridges are proposed along the Noyyal River for Ring roads, Radial Roads and Sub Arterial Road to reduce the additional travel distance

PROPOSALS FOR ECONOMIC DEVELOPMENT

  • Common facility centre for knitwear cluster at Naranapuram at a cost of 15.34 crores proposed in 2022 by the state government.
  • The knitwear hub of Tiruppur will house the first 'TIDEL Neo' in Tamil Nadu. 'TIDEL Neo' is creating 'mini' IT parks with an area of 50,000 to 1 lakh square feet in Tier 2 and Tier 3 towns with an aim to introduce the services sector across the state located at Thirumuruganpoondi in Tirupur, which will be constructed at a financial outlay of Rs 32 crores.
  • Trade Centre like Codissia Trade Centre will boost all kind of industries to reach out to the vendors and other customers.

PROPOSALS FOR GREEN PUBLIC SPACES

  • Urban Forestry is proposed for an area of 1.025 acres in Iduvoi Village
  • Parks are proposed around crematoriums
  • Nanjarayan Lake is proposed as Bird Sanctuary and its surrounding areas are proposed to be developed as park and Rs. 7 crores is allotted for Park development to forest de[partment.
  • Andipalayam Lake, Moolikulam lake are in proposals for Lakefront development with Parks, Play area and boating facilities.
  • Science Park is proposed for an area of 7 acres in Iduvoi Village

TIRUPPUR
Knitwear capital, Dollar city of India

Mission

Mission of the Tiruppur Master Plan 2041 is to cultivate a vibrant, sustainable, and inclusive urban landscape in Tiruppur, prioritizing the well-being of all residents, fostering economic prosperity, safeguarding the environment, and ensuring equitable access to opportunities."

Vision

Vision of Tiruppur Master Plan 2041 is to create a vibrant, sustainable, and inclusive urban environment in Tiruppur that enhances the quality of life for all residents, fosters economic growth, preserves the environment, and ensures equitable access to opportunities.
